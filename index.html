Welcome!

We are Biological Interdisciplinary Open Maker Environment, a student organization at Stanford University dedicated to providing anybody the skills, resources, and support to engage in a bio-hacking project. We seek to educate our membership and the broader public by expanding the scope of biotechnology beyond its conventional bounds.

BIOME is an open space for anyone to do bioengineering and biology inspired projects. Interested in getting involved? Check out our calendar for information on all of our events and workshops!
